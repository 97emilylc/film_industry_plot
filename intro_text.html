WELCOME TO FiP

A simple display and generation tool for data from the film industry, Film Industry Plots.

FiP allows the user to explore and compare plots and data about the most popular film released by the industry. The primary purpose of this website and interface is to compare the careers of male and female actors over the course of their careers. With a particular focus on how the variety of films for men is much broader than that for women.

 WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH FiP:

  • Perform a visual search for actors and actresses from the most popular 4800 films
  • Search through all films of the data set by genre, studio, title, revenue, runtime, and other components 
  • Compare via side by side bar charts of the top actors and actresses, the course of their careers with a choice of metrics
  • Dive deeper into those bar charts with a chance to analyize the data in a traditional table format as well

Look out for additional information around the site.
