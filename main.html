At a glance...

The data we are about to explore as the following attributes:

  • 4803 unique films
  • starring over 13,000 actors and actresses
  • spanning across 20 different genres
  • from as early as 1916 to recent films

 BUT HOW IS IT STORED?

  • The majority of the data is stored in two relational databases then additional relations are form in order to access comparisions between elements
    • The first database contains the values of the films and can be accessed and searched on the Movies tab above
    • The second database is the values of the actors, the values of which are spread across two different tabs, Actors for the most popular listed, and Career Tables to search for less popular individuals
